The Onahs

St. Chris Uchenna Onah is a passionate Campus Missionary who is currently serving Christ in NIFES as the Area Director of Benin Area, He just concluded his service in NIFES Uyo Area as the Area Director for 10years from (2013-2023) which covers 4 Zones in three States in Southern Nigeria encompassing 43 Campus groups. He at a time doubled as both the Area Director of Uyo Area and the Training secretary of Uyo Zone.
He got married to Stella Uchenna Onah on the 5th day of November 2011 and they are blessed with three boys. Chukwuebuka, Chidera, Chinonso (Ebenezer) Stella is a trained counselor and a passionate Teacher of God's word.
